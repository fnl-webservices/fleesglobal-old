 COMPANY OBJECTIVES

At Flees Global, we aim to build a fully integrated business with different emphasis on Asset Management of Gas, Crude and Petroleum Products Infrastructures (Pipelines and AGI’s). Thereby ensuring compliance and optimization of assets’ useful   life/extension of service life. Such as Pumps supply, Maintenance, and others. We aim at achieving this, by offering world-class services to our clients while being supported by renowned experts, companies and institutions respected all over the world.   We also aim at achieving technical integrity targets. To achieve project profit expectation and to exceed client's expectations, where possible to enable us achieve goal zero target.

COMMITMENT

We are committed to the optimization of local content in all aspects of our business and the growth of our workforce and host communities. In pursuance of our corporate goals above, the company has experienced and competent Management team in addition to a strong and Professional Workforce, complimented with technical support from our overseas partners

HEALTH,SAFETY AND ENVIRONMENT

At Flees Global, it is our utmost priority to conduct business safely, ensure good health of our staff, protect our work environment, and be sensitive to the needs of the communities around our area of operations..
